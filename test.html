Floorboards

A wooden board floor by pur natur consists of solid boards or 3-layer boards. At pur natur, you can find oak boards and Douglas fir boards. Find your personal floorboards here:

Floorboards overview

Douglas boards

Douglas boards are available as solid boards and 3-layer boards. Discover your Douglas fir floorboards of the 100mm to 400mm line, with their two beautiful sorts, Select and Natur.

Douglas floorboards

Oak boards

Oak floorboards by pur natur are available as solid oak boards or 3-layer oak boards. Discover oak boards of the 100mm to 500mm line, or your wooden board floor with mixed-width boards.

Oak floorboards

Terrace boards

Douglas terrace boards in high-quality Black Forest Douglas fir, with a smooth surface and high durability. Discover our Alpin collection with wide boards, or our Kollin collection with low knot boards.

Douglas terrace boards
